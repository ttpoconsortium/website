HR & TPO Wellness Retreat

Event Details

13th December 2025
Hyderabad
9:00 AM onwards

Description

The HR & TPO Wellness Retreat was a unique initiative designed to bring together Human Resource professionals and Training & Placement Officers for a rejuvenating experience. This event focused on mental well-being, stress management, and professional networking in a relaxed environment.

Participants engaged in wellness workshops, team-building activities, and insightful discussions on the future of recruitment and campus placements. It was an opportunity to unwind, connect with peers, and recharge for the challenges ahead.
